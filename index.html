What Passexplorer does

One platform. Three sides.

Built for fans who want in, fans who need out, and organizers who deserve more.

🔍
For fans
Explore & Buy

Discover thousands of festivals and secure your pass — from the official sale or the secondary market.

  • Search by genre, location and date
  • Compare passes across sellers
  • Every ticket verified before purchase
  • Instant digital delivery
💸
For pass holders
List & Sell

Can't make it? Sell your pass safely on the marketplace. Set your price, reach thousands of buyers.

  • List in under 2 minutes
  • Fair pricing tools built in
  • Secure payment, instant payout
  • Buyer protection on every sale
🎪
For organizers
Control & Earn

Unlike other secondary markets — you get a cut of every resale. Plus full visibility into who holds your passes.

  • Earn royalties on every resale
  • Set resale price caps if needed
  • Real-time attendee insights
  • Reduce scalping and fraud
